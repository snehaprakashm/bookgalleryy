Twilight

-Stephenie meyer

Twilight (stylized as twilight) is a 2005 young adult vampire-romance novel by author Stephenie Meyer. It is the first book in the Twilight series.

Interview with the Vampire

- Anne Rice

Interview with the Vampire is a gothic horror and vampire novel by American author Anne Rice,published in 1976.The novel centers on vampire Louis,who tells the story of his life to a reporter.

-Arthur Conan Doyle

These easy-to-read Sherlock Holmes stories provide the perfect introduction to the super sleuth and his friend and assistant.

Dracula

-Bram Stoker

Dracula is a novel by Bram Stoker, published in 1897. An epistolary novel, the narrative is related through letters, diary entries, and newspaper articles.

House of Leaves

-Mark Z. Danielewski

House of Leaves is the debut novel by American author Mark Z.Danielewski,published in March 2000 by Pantheon Books.A bestseller, it has been translated into a number of languages.
